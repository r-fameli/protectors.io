# Weapon Progressions

Weapons must be earned at level-up events, excluding the default Turret (all players start with it).
Level 1 = acquiring the weapon. Each subsequent level is an upgrade.
Max weapon level is 4 (base + 3 upgrades).

## Turret

Players all start with the 'Turret' weapon by default.

1. A turret weapon that fires periodically.
2. Increased fire rate
3. Increased damage
4. Increased radius
<!-- 5. Triple shot -->

## Spiderweb

1. Slows enemies in an area and deals damage over time to a single enemy.
2. Increased damage
3. Increased radius + slow
4. Increased fire rate (spider attack speed)
<!-- 5. Two spiders deployed -->

## Crossbow

1. Pierces enemies.
2. Increased range + travel distance
3. Increased fire rate
4. Increased damage
<!-- 5. Increased travel distance -->

## Springer

1. Deploys caltrops periodically that hurt enemies that step on them.
2. Increased fire rate (caltrop deployment rate)
3. Increased radius (caltrop spread area)
4. Increased damage (stronger caltrops)
